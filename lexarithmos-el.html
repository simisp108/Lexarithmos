About us

Τι είναι ο Λεξάριθμος

Lexarithmos is a private institute teaching Mathematics and Greek to children ranging from Grade 4 to Grade 6 in primary school up. Our aim is to prepare students in the best way possible and aid them in achieving their goals, either preparing them for public, or private secondary school.

Our name comes from the concept of "Lexarithm", where each letter has an innate value, with each word's Lexarithm being the total value of all its letters. To calculate this sum, the word must be written in capital letters and using the table of values for each capital letter, one can find the Lexarithm of a word. As far as we know, this concept is exclusive to the Greek language.

The Lexarithm of the word "Lexarithmos" is 525
